## Eiffel Tower

- Located in Paris, France
- Constructed from 1887 to 1889 for 1889 World's Fair, as its entrance
- 1063 feet tall

Originally criticized by France's leading artists and intellectuals, has since become an adored cultural icon

![Tower of Pisa](/assets/buildings/pisa.jpg)

## Tower of Pisa

- Located in Pisa, Italy
- Constructed 1173--1372
- 183.27 feet tall on low side, 185.93 on high side

Its famous tilt began during construction, and increased until stabilized in late 20th and early 21st centuries

## Statue of Liberty

- Located in New York Harbor, New York City
- Work began in early 1870s, dedicated October 28, 1886
- 151 feet and 1 inch tall (to torch)

Gift from France to the United States, built by Gustave Eiffel. Original name (in French): _Liberty Enlightening the World_

## Taj Mahal

- Located in Agra, Uttar Pradesh, India
- Constructed 1632--1653, though was essentially finished 10 years earlier
- 240 feet tall, 17 hectares in area

Means "Crown of the Palace", comissioned by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife
